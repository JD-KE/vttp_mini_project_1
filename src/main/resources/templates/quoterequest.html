Request for Quotes

Here is where users can make a request for quotes to contractors who are able install Solar PV Systems onto your owned building(s). Please fill in your contact details as well as answer a few simple questions, which will be provided to the contractors to intiate the intial design and quotation process. They will later contact you to arrange a site visit to see how to best design the system, after which they will provide a quote for you to review. You will be provided a tracking number to track your request.

If you are planning to contact contractors you know directly, you can still fill up the form and download the form details after registration for easy reference. Just check the do not contact option.
